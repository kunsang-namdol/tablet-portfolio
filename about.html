ABOUT US

Our Story

Sweet Tarts Cafe is a family-owned cafe that serves freshly brewed coffee and other cozy beverages, alongside a wide range of scrumptious pastries from local bakeries.


With both indoor and outdoor seating, customers are able to enjoy the lovely summer sun or our toasty fireplace in the winter. Our door seating features


Inspired by the rich culture and cozy atmosphere of Parisian patisseries and cafes on a trip to France, the Lunz family felt the need to share a taste of the European culture and cafe dining experience with their hometown in Chicago. Hence, Sweet Tarts Cafe was born.
